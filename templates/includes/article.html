Overview

The Anomaly Based Intrusion Detection System (ANIDS) leverages ensemble machine learning techniques to identify and mitigate potential threats in real-time. By analyzing network traffic patterns and user behaviors, ANIDS can effectively distinguish between legitimate activities and potential intrusions.

Key Features

  • Real-time threat detection and response
  • Adaptive learning capabilities
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics
